Our Story

On May 9th, 2010 we opened the doors to Fantastico. The culmination of years of dreaming and imagination, Fantastico was a minimalist modern vision that put the focal point on the cuisine and dining as an experience.
Till these years pur resturant has become a successful and quite popular among the citizens here and all credit goes to our dedicated and smart team. Fantastico believes that consumers experiences are critical proposition which leads to sustained growth of a business. Building on this basic pillar, a positive consumers experience can build brand, loyalty and affinity, evangelize the food-tech product or service, leaving behind a positive customer review to develop a retention in the business revenue model.
